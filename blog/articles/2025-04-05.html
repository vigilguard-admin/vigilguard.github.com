Strong Password

Strong passwords are a basic but powerful way to protect your digital life. Weak passwords are easily guessed and reused passwords increase your risk.

To create a strong password:

- Use 12+ characters
- Combine upper/lowercase letters
- Add numbers and special symbols (!, @, #)
- Avoid personal info or simple words
- Never reuse passwords on different sites

Examples:

- S@f3tyF1r$t_2025!
- L0ngF\Str0ng_P@ss!

Passphrases are also great: “MyD0g_E@ts_B33f!”. Use a password manager to store them securely, and always enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) when possible.
