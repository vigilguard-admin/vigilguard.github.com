Use Strong and Unique Passwords

Using strong and unique passwords is essential for protecting your online accounts. Weak passwords are easily cracked by cybercriminals.

- Create passwords with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols
- Avoid using common phrases or personal information
- Use a different password for each account
- Consider using a password manager to store passwords securely

Strong passwords are your first line of defense against unauthorized access. Keep your accounts secure by following these guidelines.
