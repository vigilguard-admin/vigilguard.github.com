Be Careful on Public Wi-Fi

Public Wi-Fi in cafés, airports, or hotels may seem convenient, but it's often unsecured and risky. Hackers can intercept your data easily.

- Avoid accessing banking or sensitive accounts
- Don’t enter passwords or personal info
- Use a VPN for a secure connection
- Turn off file sharing and auto-connect features

Always assume public networks are being watched. If it’s not urgent, wait until you're on a trusted, secure network.
