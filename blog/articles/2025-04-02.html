Do Not Install Random Software from Email

Installing software from unknown email links is a major security risk. Cybercriminals often disguise malware as helpful tools or urgent updates.

- Never trust email attachments from unknown senders
- Don’t click “install” links without verifying the source
- Use official websites or trusted app stores
- Report suspicious emails to your IT team

One careless click can lead to data theft, ransomware, or system compromise. Stay alert and only install software you trust.
