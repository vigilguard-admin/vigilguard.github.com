Do Not Click on Unknown Email Links

Links in emails from unknown senders can be dangerous. They may lead to phishing sites or trigger malware downloads.

- Don’t click links from people you don’t recognize
- Hover over links to preview the URL
- Watch for misspelled domains or strange addresses
- Report suspicious emails to your IT team

Even one wrong click can compromise your system. When in doubt, delete the email or verify the sender first.
