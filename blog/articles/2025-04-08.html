Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Two-factor authentication (2FA) adds an extra layer of security to your accounts. It requires a second form of verification beyond just a password.

- Use 2FA on all accounts that offer it
- Choose methods like SMS, authenticator apps, or security keys
- Ensure your backup methods are secure
- Regularly review and update your 2FA settings

By enabling 2FA, you significantly reduce the risk of unauthorized access, even if your password is compromised.
